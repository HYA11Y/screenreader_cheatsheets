About Us

Cactus is a type of plant that can store large amounts of water and survive in extremely hot and dry habitats. There are around 2000 different species of cactus that differ in size, shape, color and type of habitat. Almost all cacti are native to deserts and dry regions of South and North America.Learn more about the variaties of cactus. Or, if you're more into the cactus's living conditions, check out Wikipedia's desert page.
